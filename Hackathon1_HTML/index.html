We import high-end wines from all over the world to bring to your doorstep.
Our mission is to make sure you have the best possible experience while tasting your wine, therefore we also provide tailored recommendations based on your taste profile regarding food pairing and even scenario setting.

This is how we work

Your tailored experience begins right here in our website, by filling in the from on the subscribe page.
After you fill this out, we'll create a taste profile based on your answers and provide you with our recommendations which you can order on a monthly basis. In your profile you can highlight the types / grapes of wine you want to exclude, the ones you really want to include and any allergies regarding the food pairing recommendations.

With every order you'll receive and email in which we kindly ask for your feedback, based on this we will finetune your profile and assure a tailor made experience.
